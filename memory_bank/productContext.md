# Product Context

## Product Description
PlayListify is a cross-platform music playlist management application that bridges the gap between different music streaming services. It allows users to create, manage, and share playlists that can be synchronized across multiple platforms.

## Key Features
1. **Unified Playlist Management** - Create and edit playlists that sync across connected streaming services
2. **Cross-Platform Integration** - Connect with major streaming platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, etc.
3. **Social Sharing** - Share playlists with friends and discover playlists from other users
4. **Smart Recommendations** - Get suggestions for songs to add based on your listening history
5. **Playlist Analytics** - View insights about your playlists and listening habits

## User Personas

### Casual Listener (Primary)
- Uses multiple streaming services
- Creates playlists occasionally
- Wants an easy way to share music with friends

### Music Curator (Secondary)
- Creates carefully crafted playlists regularly
- Shares playlists with a following
- Needs detailed management tools

### Social Sharer (Tertiary)
- Primarily interested in sharing music with friends
- Discovers new music through social connections
- Values the social aspects of music

## Competitive Landscape
- **Direct Competitors**: Other playlist management tools (SongShift, Soundiiz)
- **Indirect Competitors**: Native playlist features in streaming services
- **Differentiators**: Focus on social sharing and cross-platform synchronization

## User Journey
1. User signs up and connects streaming accounts
2. Creates or imports existing playlists
3. Edits and organizes playlists
4. Shares playlists with friends or community
5. Discovers new playlists from other users
6. Receives recommendations based on preferences
